about KJSCE

 

K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering, located within the Somaiya campus at Vidyavihar, Mumbai is graded as 'A' by the NBA (National Board Of Accreditation). The Somaiya campus has more than 20,000 students in diverse disciplines like Engineering, Management, Commerce, Arts and Polytechnic. To cater to such a humongous need, we have a sprawling campus of over 65 acres in Vidyavihar, in the heart of Mumbai. A feather has been added to our cap, when we became the first Self-financed (private) Engineering College afiliated to University of Mumbai to get an Autonomous status. From the academic year 2014-15 our College has "Academic Autonomy", while continuing to be afiliated to University of Mumbai.

about SKREAM

 

Skream, the annual sports meet of K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering, in its third year is now a popular sports festival. About SKREAM

Skream, the annual sports meet of K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering, in its third year is now a popular sports festival. Consisting of multiple events such as football, cricket, squash and kabaddi, Skream is not just a festival but a lifestyle for Somaiyaites. Witnessing innovative and exciting events, colossal crowds and eminent personalities, Skream is the ultimate place to be for all sportspersons. So it is time to SKREAM again!
